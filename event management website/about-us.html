Who We Are

We love to provide our clients with the right resources to create a successful event. Our extensive range of events and activities are designed to ‘bring people together’ which we believe is paramount to any successful organisation in an increasingly competitive market. We are a professional event and conference management company, based at Panchkula and Mumbai and operating nationally. Some of our key accounts are ICICI Bank, ICICI ,HDFC Bank, HDFC Ergo General Insurance, ICICI Prudential , ICICI Lombard, Aditya Birla Sun Life Insurance, Tata AIA and Intas Pharmacetical Company to name a few.

Whether you have a MICE event, domestic or conference / event, a launch event or a press meet, an awards night, sales conference, promotional event or you want to simply incentivize your employees for their achievements, we can design something totally unique that will meet both your budget and brief, and will guarantee to leave lasting memories for all the right reasons. We have delivered memorable solutions anywhere in the country

We are committed to producing bespoke conferences and events to the highest quality. We aim to surpass the expectations of our clients and our attendees. We go the extra mile to deliver engaging events which inspire, provoke action and deliver results. Conferences are our specialty and passion! Since setting up the company in 2000 we have produced numerous conferences ranging from focus groups for 50 people through to conferences for over 5000 people, as well as outdoor public music concerts and events for up to 6,000 visitors.
